UCL

The UEFA Champions League (UCL) is the most prestigious club competition in European football.

Established in 1955 as the European Champion Clubs' Cup, it was rebranded as the UEFA Champions League in 1992. The tournament features top-division European clubs competing for the coveted title. Real Madrid holds the record for the most UCL titles, with 15 victories as of 2026.
